## Name
Cookie Monster

## Short description (132 chars max)
Automatically removes ad trackers. Keeps your logins, carts, and subscriptions. Configures to how you actually browse.

## Full description

Cookie Monster watches every site you visit and automatically deletes the cookies that track you — without breaking the things you actually need.

**What it does**

Every time you load a page, Cookie Monster scans what got stored in your browser. Known ad trackers from networks like DoubleClick, Rubicon, and AppNexus are deleted automatically. Suspicious unknown cookies are flagged for your review. First-party cookies and cookies you rely on are left completely alone.

**Configures to how you browse**

On first install, a short questionnaire asks about your browsing habits. Do you use "Login with Google"? Do you have paid subscriptions? Do you use live chat on websites? Cookie Monster uses your answers to build a personal whitelist — so your social logins, shopping carts, subscription access, and display preferences are never touched.

You can update these settings anytime from the extension popup.

**What you see**

- A badge showing how many suspicious cookies are waiting for review
- A popup grouped by site — see every flagged cookie across every site you've visited
- A deletion log showing everything that was automatically removed
- Per-site and global "Delete All" buttons
- A Safe tab showing recognized legitimate cookies

**Three protection modes**

- Auto-delete + notify (recommended): known trackers are deleted silently, a notification tells you what was removed
- Flag only: nothing is deleted automatically — suspicious cookies appear in the popup for you to review and delete manually
- Maximum protection: everything flagged is deleted automatically

**Completely private**

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device. No account required. No servers. Cookie Monster never phones home.
